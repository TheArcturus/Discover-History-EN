Vietnam War

The Vietnam War also called the american war is a conflict that has divided for decades the North and South of Vietnam.


Indochina wars

The war in Vietnam started after the Indochina War that caused the division of Indochina in a lot of states like Laos, Cambodia, North Vietnam and South Vietnam. The conflict began mainly because North Vietnam wanted to unify the whole country under their communist government, while the South wanted to follow an Eastern, liberal regime.

The Cold War

The Cold war design a state of political hostility between the United States and USSR that was characterized by threats, propaganda, roundabout conflicts. The Vietnam War was a product of the Cold War that began 45 years before because the South of Vietnam was influenced by the USA while the North of Vietnam was influenced by the USSR. During the war, 500,000 U.S. military personnel were stationed in Vietnam to help the South to fight against the North and USSR was helping the North by providing troops and support.


The consequences

The war has affected people by separating families for decades, making some citizens to leave the country (boat people) and also the land because a large part of the country is heavily damaged because of the past battles.
