India in 1632

The Birth of an historical building

One of the most relevant element that happened in India in 1632 is definitely the edification of a world-known monument that now belongs to the indian heritage, the Taj Mahal.


The history behind the erection

This monument was built in Agra in the north of India by Shâh Jahân who was at this time the Mughal Empire sovereign. The emperor who has lost his wife Ara Mahal the year before, when she was giving birth, has decided to construct an outstanding mausoleum that would represent as the beauty. He wanted to build a majestic tombs where his deceased wife will rest forever and show to the rest of the world how much he cared for her.


The architectural style of the monument

The monument built in a total of 42-acres in surface blends, the Indian, Persian and Islamic style, which was very representative of the cultural diversity of India. It contains several gardens, two twins mosque and a museum, all symmetrically positioned.
