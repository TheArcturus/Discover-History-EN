The winning back of Spain which is called « La Reconquista » in spanish took place in the eighteenth century it began in 722 and ended in January 1492


Spain before the Reconquista

Spain was Saracen in the Middle Ages which means that she was invaded by North Africans’ inhabitants whose goals were to gain territories on the one hand and to perhaps convert spanish people who were christian to Islam on the other hand. Saracen has been occupying a great part of Spain (which is named Andalusia ) when christians monarch decided to get it back .


Seven centuries of battles

We can count a lot of battles like Covadonga or Cantabrique The last battle which took place in Grenada allowed christian to get back their territories. Even if the muslim empire was defeated their influence is still present :their architecture,art, traditions .. for instance we can find a lot of mosque (where muslim used to pray their God) one of the most famous is located in Cordoue. Spain is now, like many countries in Europe, a blend of two heritages.
