Bastille Day

The Bastille Day in July the 14th is the national day and represents the first day of the French revolution. Three main elements happened this day: The Hotel of Invalids has been attacked and robbed in order to get firearms and cannons for the rebellion The French Guards rally the movement and helped the population to attack the Bastille The constituent assembly has voted for the abolition of feudalism, which signs the end of Monarchy in France



The abolition of feudalism

On the 4th of August the constituent assembly voted for the abolition of feudalism, which signs the end of Monarchy and nobility in France after centuries.



The history behind those events

With the raising of the number of Bourgeois which are in France decades before, and the economic crisis due to food shortage that was happening during that time, the anger against privilege and the nobility in general, was felt by the majority of the French population When on July the 11 the finance minister of the king, loved by the poorest has been dismissed, the anger was too much and Parisians has decided to rebel against the King and attack the royal prison, the Bastille.
